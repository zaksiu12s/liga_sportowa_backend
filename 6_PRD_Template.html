Executive Summary * **[Brief]:** A high-level overview of the project, its purpose, and expected impact. Goals & Objectives (SMART) * **[Brief/PRD]:** What specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound outcomes do we expect from this project? How will we know if it's successful? *(Example: * **Increase** new user signup completion rate from 60% to 75% within 3 months post-launch. * **Reduce** support tickets related to onboarding confusion by 20% within 2 months post-launch. * **Improve** the average time-to-first-value (e.g., first completed task) by 15% for new users.)* --- ### 4. Target Audience * **[Brief/PRD]:** Who are the primary users/customers for whom this solution is being built? What are their key characteristics, needs, and pain points related to this project? *(Example: New users (first-time visitors, free trial sign-ups) who are unfamiliar with our platform's core features. They are typically busy professionals looking for quick solutions and clear guidance.)* --- ### 5. Scope * **[Brief/PRD]:** What is **in scope** for this project? What specific features, functionalities, or areas are we focusing on? * What is **out of scope** (for now)? Key Features & Functionality * **[Brief/PRD]:** List the core features and functionalities that will be developed.
